Publishing and Sharing Reports in Power BI

Introduction

This tutorial covers how to publish and share reports in Power BI, including publishing to Power BI Service and sharing reports with others.

Publishing to Power BI Service

Learn how to publish your reports to the Power BI Service:

  1. Click on "Publish" in Power BI Desktop.
  2. Select your workspace in the Power BI Service.
  3. Click "Select" to publish your report.
  4. Configure scheduled refresh for your dataset if needed.

Sharing Reports

Learn how to share your reports with others:

  1. Open the Power BI Service and navigate to your report.
  2. Click on the "Share" button.
  3. Enter the email addresses of the people you want to share with and set permissions.
  4. Click "Share" to send the invitation.
  5. Optionally, create a shareable link for broader distribution.

Implementing Security

Learn how to secure your reports:

  1. Set up Row-Level Security (RLS) to restrict data access based on user roles.
  2. Use Power BI Premium features for more advanced security options.
  3. Regularly review and update access permissions.
