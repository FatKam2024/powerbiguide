Lab 4: Dashboard Creation

Objective

In this lab, you will learn how to create a dashboard in Power BI Service using the report you created in Lab 3.

Steps

  1. Publish your report to Power BI Service:
    • In Power BI Desktop, click "Publish" in the Home tab.
    • Select a workspace to publish to (create a new one if necessary).
    • Click "Select" to publish your report.
  2. Open Power BI Service (app.powerbi.com) and navigate to your published report.
  3. Create a new dashboard:
    • Click the "+" icon next to "Dashboards" in the left navigation pane.
    • Name your dashboard "Sales Overview".
  4. Pin visualizations to your dashboard:
    • Open your report in Power BI Service.
    • Hover over a visualization and click the pin icon.
    • Select your "Sales Overview" dashboard and click "Pin".
    • Repeat for at least four visualizations.
  5. Customize the dashboard layout:
    • Resize and rearrange the tiles on your dashboard.
    • Add a text box with a dashboard title and description.
  6. Add a web content tile:
    • Click "Add tile" in the top menu.
    • Select "Web content" and add a relevant website (e.g., your company's homepage).

Exercise

  1. Create a dashboard named "Sales Overview" with at least six tiles:
    • Four visualizations from your report
    • One text box with title and description
    • One web content tile
  2. Arrange the tiles in a logical and visually appealing layout.
  3. Set a custom theme for your dashboard.
  4. Configure your dashboard for mobile view.
  5. Share your dashboard with a colleague (or instructor) and set appropriate permissions.
