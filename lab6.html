Lab 6: Publishing and Sharing Reports

Objective

In this lab, you will learn how to publish reports from Power BI Desktop to the Power BI Service, and how to share them with others.

Steps

Part 1: Publishing Reports

  1. Open your Power BI report in Power BI Desktop.
  2. Click on "Publish" in the Home tab.
  3. Select your workspace in Power BI Service (create a new one if necessary).
  4. Click "Select" to publish your report.
  5. Wait for the publishing process to complete and note the success message.

Part 2: Sharing Reports

  1. Open Power BI Service (app.powerbi.com) and navigate to your published report.
  2. Click on the "Share" button in the top menu.
  3. Enter the email addresses of people you want to share the report with.
  4. Set appropriate permissions (View or Edit).
  5. Optionally, add a message to the recipients.
  6. Click "Share" to send the invitations.

Exercise

  1. Publish your report from Power BI Desktop to Power BI Service.
  2. In Power BI Service, create a copy of your report and make some changes (e.g., add a new visualization).
  3. Share your original report with a colleague or your instructor, giving them "View" permissions.
  4. Create a shareable link for your report that doesn't require a Power BI account to view.
  5. Set up a scheduled refresh for your dataset (if it's connected to a data source that supports this feature).
  6. Explore the Version History feature of your report in Power BI Service.

Additional Challenge

For those who finish early or want an extra challenge:

  • Create a Power BI app from your workspace and publish it.
  • Set up row-level security (RLS) for your report and test it with different user roles.
  • Use the Analyze in Excel feature to connect your published dataset to Excel.
