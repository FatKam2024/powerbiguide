Creating Reports in Power BI

Introduction

This tutorial covers how to create reports in Power BI, including adding visualizations, formatting, and interactivity.

Adding Visualizations

Learn how to add different types of visualizations to your report:

  1. Drag fields from the Fields pane onto the report canvas.
  2. Select the type of visualization from the Visualizations pane.
  3. Customize the visualization using the Format pane.
  4. Use custom visuals from the AppSource to extend your options.

Formatting Visualizations

Learn how to format your visualizations to enhance readability:

  1. Use the Format pane to adjust colors, fonts, and sizes.
  2. Add titles and labels to your visualizations.
  3. Apply themes for consistent formatting across your report.
  4. Use conditional formatting to highlight important data points.

Adding Interactivity

Learn how to add interactivity to your reports:

  1. Use slicers to allow users to filter data.
  2. Enable cross-filtering and cross-highlighting between visuals.
  3. Add bookmarks to save and share specific views of your report.
  4. Enable drill-through to navigate between report pages.
